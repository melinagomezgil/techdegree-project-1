Experience

I have 10 years of experience as an ESL teacher. I´ve taught English as a second language to adults, teenagers and children in both individual and group settings. I´ve worked for many prestigious bilingual schools in Medellin like The Columbus School.

Hobbies

Writing and amateur photography are also hobbies I enjoy. In my free time, I immerse myself in nature photography and black and white portraits of people and animals.
